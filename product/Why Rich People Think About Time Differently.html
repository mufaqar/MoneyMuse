SCENE 05 Opportunity cost of time — the real cost of doing it yourself
Voiceover
Every hour you spend doing something is an hour you're not doing something else. That's opportunity cost. But if you could be earning $100 an hour doing your actual work, that $50 "saving" just cost you $500 in lost income. Rich people don't ask "how much does this cost?" They ask "what else could I be doing with this time?"
