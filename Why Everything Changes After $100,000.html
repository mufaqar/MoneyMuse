Video Description

There's a number that quietly changes everything about how you think about money. It's not $1 million. It's not $500,000. It's $100,000. In this video, we break down the psychological shift that happens when your net worth hits six figures — and why the real transformation has nothing to do with the money itself.

We explore the scarcity-to-growth mindset flip, the compound interest tipping point, the social pressure trap, and why the first $100,000 is the hardest but most important financial milestone you'll ever hit.

If you're working toward financial independence, this video will show you what actually changes when you cross the $100,000 threshold — and how to prepare for it before you get there.
